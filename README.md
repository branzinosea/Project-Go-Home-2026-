# Project: Go Home (2026)
> **"Get Home Alive."**

**BRANZINO** is an integrated hemostatic and tissue management system designed to treat **massive junctional hemorrhage** (inguinal and axillary) where standard tourniquets cannot be applied

## 1. Core Philosophy: "One-Shot & Run"
**Kinematic Repurposing**: Transforms standard-issue infantry knee/elbow pads into high-pressure hemostatic chamber walls.
* **Low-Cost Material**: Utilizes high-tensile agricultural PO/PE greenhouse film to bypass specialized medical supply chain instabilities.
* **Minimal Cognitive Load**: Designed to eliminate manual pressure titration, allowing a **"Shoot & Run"** doctrine through automated mechanical pressure regulation and full-volume discharge.

## 2. Technical Specifications
**Triple Sequential V-Valves**: A springless, cam-over sliding mechanism with precise **0.2–0.3 mm clearances** to automatically maintain arterial tamponade pressure and vent excess gas
**Branzino Pepper (Gelation)**: A granular catalyst (Thrombin/Chitosan) that triggers a hyper-hemostatic state, forming a macroscopic viscoelastic bio-plug within seconds
**Lubricated Interface**: PEG-integrated polymers synthesize a low-friction film upon contact with blood, preventing tissue integration and allowing **En Bloc extraction** (like a cork) without traumatic tissue scraping in a surgical facility

## 3. Field Execution Protocol
**Phase 1 (Measurement)**: Individual limb circumference measurement using $C=2\pi r$ to derive optimal incision lengths from the D-Chart].
**Phase 2 (Pre-setting)**: Physical pre-scoring (Hard-Wired) for high-lethality critical zones like the inguinal artery to ensure autonomous bursting.
***Phase 3 (V-Groove Guide)**: Carving a micro-channel guide over marker lines to prevent blade deviation caused by tremors during high-stress scenarios.
