# Project: Go Home (2026)
> "Get Home Alive."

**BRANZINO** and the **TJHESS-E** system represent an integrated hemostatic and tissue management architecture designed to treat massive junctional hemorrhage (inguinal and axillary) where standard tourniquets cannot be applied.

---

## 1. Core Philosophy: "One-Shot & Run"

* **Kinematic Repurposing:** Transforms standard-issue infantry knee/elbow pads into high-pressure hemostatic chamber walls.
* **Low-Cost Material:** Utilizes high-tensile agricultural PO/PE greenhouse film to bypass specialized medical supply chain instabilities.
* **Minimal Cognitive Load:** Designed to eliminate manual pressure titration, allowing a "Shoot & Run" doctrine through automated mechanical pressure regulation and full-volume discharge.

## 2. Technical Specifications (The BRANZINO Standard)

* **Triple Sequential V-Valves:** A springless, cam-over sliding mechanism with precise 0.2–0.3 mm clearances to automatically maintain arterial tamponade pressure and vent excess gas.
* **Branzino Pepper (Gelation):** A granular catalyst (Thrombin/Chitosan) that triggers a hyper-hemostatic state, forming a macroscopic viscoelastic bio-plug within seconds.
* **Lubricated Interface:** PEG-integrated polymers synthesize a low-friction film upon contact with blood, preventing tissue integration.

## 3. Tactical Execution Sequence (The TJHESS-E Kit)

The TJHESS-E implementation standardizes the BRANZINO architecture into a 30-second mechanical survival kit.

* **Phase 1 (0-5 Seconds):** High-Pressure Chemical Debridement via primary nozzle to disperse contaminants and numb peripheral nerves.
* **Phase 2 (5-15 Seconds):** Application of the multi-layer casing and mechanical lock using a unidirectional Ratchet Tie-Lock for external compression.
* **Phase 3 (15-25 Seconds):** Exothermic Tamponade injection of polyurethane foam (38-40°C) to penetrate deep severed arteries and mitigate the Lethal Triad.
* **Phase 4 (25-30 Seconds):** Systemic Shock Prevention via Fentanyl transmucosal patch and immediate tactical egress (Shoot & Run).

## 4. Definitive Care: Surgical "Twist & Release"

* **Objective:** Non-traumatic removal of hemostatic foam at a Role 2/3 surgical facility.
* **Mechanism:** Lateral bilateral twisting motion applied to the external shield.
* **Shear Force Separation:** Friction between the multi-layer gauze collapses, allowing the foam plug to drop out cleanly (En Bloc) without evulsing raw tissue.

## 5. ⚠️ WARNING / DANGER (CRITICAL)

* **Life Over Limb:** This system prioritizes absolute survival over limb preservation. The exothermic foam may cause local thermal injuries, accepted as a necessary tactical trade-off to prevent immediate exsanguination and hypothermia.
* **Abdominal Contraindication:** NEVER inject foam into abdominal eviscerations or penetrating abdominal cavities. Utilize the vinyl guide and multi-layer gauze only as a protective bowel bag without foam injection.
