# Project: Go Home (2026)
> **"Get Home Alive."**

[cite_start]**BRANZINO** is an integrated hemostatic and tissue management system designed to treat **massive junctional hemorrhage** (inguinal and axillary) where standard tourniquets cannot be applied[cite: 67, 114].

## 1. Core Philosophy: "One-Shot & Run"
* [cite_start]**Kinematic Repurposing**: Transforms standard-issue infantry knee/elbow pads into high-pressure hemostatic chamber walls[cite: 65, 68].
* [cite_start]**Low-Cost Material**: Utilizes high-tensile agricultural PO/PE greenhouse film to bypass specialized medical supply chain instabilities[cite: 78].
* [cite_start]**Minimal Cognitive Load**: Designed to eliminate manual pressure titration, allowing a **"Shoot & Run"** doctrine through automated mechanical pressure regulation and full-volume discharge[cite: 69, 70].

## 2. Technical Specifications
* [cite_start]**Triple Sequential V-Valves**: A springless, cam-over sliding mechanism with precise **0.2–0.3 mm clearances** to automatically maintain arterial tamponade pressure and vent excess gas[cite: 83, 85, 88].
* [cite_start]**Branzino Pepper (Gelation)**: A granular catalyst (Thrombin/Chitosan) that triggers a hyper-hemostatic state, forming a macroscopic viscoelastic bio-plug within seconds[cite: 117, 118, 120].
* [cite_start]**Lubricated Interface**: PEG-integrated polymers synthesize a low-friction film upon contact with blood, preventing tissue integration and allowing **En Bloc extraction** (like a cork) without traumatic tissue scraping in a surgical facility[cite: 123, 124, 136].

## 3. Field Execution Protocol
* [cite_start]**Phase 1 (Measurement)**: Individual limb circumference measurement using $C=2\pi r$ to derive optimal incision lengths from the D-Chart[cite: 146, 147].
* [cite_start]**Phase 2 (Pre-setting)**: Physical pre-scoring (Hard-Wired) for high-lethality critical zones like the inguinal artery to ensure autonomous bursting[cite: 149, 151, 152].
* [cite_start]**Phase 3 (V-Groove Guide)**: Carving a micro-channel guide over marker lines to prevent blade deviation caused by tremors during high-stress scenarios[cite: 157, 158, 159].
